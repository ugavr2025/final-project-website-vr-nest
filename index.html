UGA VR Final Project

VR Nest: Multiplayer Museum Adventure

Build a living museum with themed exhibits, conversational NPCs, and cooperative scavenger hunts that prime players for each gallery. Every hallway teaches, every hidden clue foreshadows, and every exhibit celebrates interactive storytelling in full 3D.

Unity + VR • 2025 Multiplayer | Scavenger Hunt | 3D Sculptures

Project Description

VR Nest creates a museum where every wing blends narrative discovery with collaborative scavenger hunts. Players travel together through curated corridors, meeting NPC docents who explain exhibit themes while planting the clues that guide teams toward the next sculpture hall. On the way, teammates search for artifacts, translate environmental cues, and unlock diegetic waypoints that echo the story of the destination gallery.

Each exhibit spotlights a fully 3D object or installation—kinetic sculptures, spatial sound totems, or interactive relics—so visitors engage with depth, scale, and material. The experience doubles as a sandbox for experimenting with AI-driven narration, smart sensing hooks, and accessibility-friendly guidance tuned for cooperative VR play.

Experience Highlights

Story-Rich NPCs

Custom docents deliver lore, tips, and foreshadowing, keeping the museum cohesive while giving every player a reason to listen before rushing ahead.

Co-op Scavenger Hunts

Layered clue chains send teams across the museum floor plan, rewarding observation, note sharing, and synchronous problem solving.

3D Sculpture Focus

Every hunt culminates with a hero asset—interactive sculptures and spatial installations that showcase material detail and depth cues.

Research Sandbox

Supports experimentation with LLM-driven dialog, mobile sensing inputs, and smart fitness insights inside shared VR spaces.
